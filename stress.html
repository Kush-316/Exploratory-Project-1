Welcome to Stress-Strain Error Estimation Model

We have created a model that helps you find the Mean Absolute Error(MAE) from the stress strain curve. The model uses ANN and is an application of deep learning and gives extremely accurate value. It is a part of a larger exploratory project taken under Prof. Srihari Dodla, IIT BHU.
