How the Model Works?

We have created a model that detects whether a patient is infected with CoVID-19 by analyzing the CT scan of the patient uploaded by the user. The model is an application of deep learning and uses CNN for detection. Our goal is to minimize the time required for administrative work and data analysis by healthcare experts.

Precautions

Wash hands on regular intervals

Handwashing with soap removes germs from hands. This helps prevent infections because: People frequently touch their eyes, nose, and mouth without even realizing it.

Quit Smoking

When you quit smoking, your immune system is no longer exposed to tar and nicotine. It will become stronger, and you will be less likely to get sick.

Get your protection

It protects you and your child from many serious and potentially deadly diseases. It helps to make community healthy and ready to fight diseases

Get regular checkups

It's no secret that the longer a disease or sickness has to develop, the worse it gets. Regular check-ups with a family physician can help diagnose a disease before it spreads and turns into a serious condition.

Eat healthy

A healthy diet typically includes foods from all of the major food groups, including fruits and vegetables of many colors.It includes eating nutritious foods.

Keep your immune system strong

Our immune system, a network of intricate stages and pathways in the body, protects us against these harmful microbes as well as certain diseases.
