Welcome to Pneumonia Detection Model

We have created a model that detects whether a patient is infected with Pneumonia by analyzing the Chest X-Ray scan of the patient uploaded by the user. The model is an application of deep learning and uses CNN for detection. Our goal is to minimize the time required for administrative work and data analysis by healthcare experts.
